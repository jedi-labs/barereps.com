Common questions

How do I restore my purchase?

Open Bare Reps → Profile tab → scroll down → tap "Restore Purchase". Make sure you're signed in to the same Apple ID used to originally subscribe.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Go to iOS Settings → your name → Subscriptions → Bare Reps → Cancel. Your Pro access continues until the end of the current billing period.

Will my progress be lost if I delete the app?

Yes — Bare Reps stores everything locally on your device with no cloud backup. Deleting the app permanently removes your workout history and streak.

Will there be an Android version?

Not at launch. V1 is iOS only. Once the iOS app covers its costs, Android is next.

Something in the app is broken. How do I report it?

Email hello@jedilabs.io with a description and your iOS version. Screenshots help. We'll get back to you fast.
