Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Bare Reps is a product of Jedi Advisory. It is built on a simple principle: your data is yours. The app works entirely on your device — there is no server, no account, and nothing leaves your phone.

What we collect

Nothing. Bare Reps does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All information you enter — your name, workout history, streak, and progress — is stored locally on your device using iOS system storage and is never shared with us or any third party.

What stays on your device

  • Your profile name
  • Workout history and stats (exercises completed, duration, calories)
  • Weekly streak and goal settings
  • Difficulty progression per workout category
  • Reminder preferences
  • Custom rep overrides

This data is stored using AsyncStorage, a local on-device key-value store. It is not backed up to iCloud and is deleted if you uninstall the app.

Notifications

Bare Reps uses local notifications — reminders scheduled on your device — to help you stay consistent. These are generated entirely on-device. No notification tokens or identifiers are sent to any server.

Payments

Subscriptions are processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not see or store your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs payment data.

Third-party services

Bare Reps does not integrate any analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. There are no third-party services with access to your usage or data.

Children

Bare Reps is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change our data practices, we will update this page and revise the "Last updated" date above. Any future changes will maintain the same commitment to not collecting your personal data.
